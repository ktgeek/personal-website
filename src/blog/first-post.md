This is the first post on this site. I've been meaning to start a blog for a while, and
this is me finally doing it.

## Why a blog?

A few reasons:

1. **Writing clarifies thinking.** If I can't explain something clearly, I probably don't understand it well enough.
2. **A record of what I've learned.** Past me would have benefited from the notes I never wrote down.
3. **It might help someone.** Even a small audience makes the effort worthwhile.

## What I'll write about

Mostly software: things I've built, patterns I've found useful, tools I like, and the occasional opinion.
I'll try to keep posts focused and practical.

## The setup

This site is built with [Eleventy](https://www.11ty.dev/) and styled with [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/).
It's minimal by design — no analytics, no newsletter, no comments. Just text and code.
